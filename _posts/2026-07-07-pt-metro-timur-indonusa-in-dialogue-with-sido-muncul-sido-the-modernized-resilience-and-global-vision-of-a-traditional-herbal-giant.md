At a time when Southeast Asian consumer market is undergoing deep transformation, how can traditional industries remain evergreen through modern management and technological innovation? Recently, PT Metro Timur Indonusa held an in-depth dialogue with Dr. Irwan Hidayat, President Director of SIDO, a benchmark Indonesian national brand and traditional herbal medicine giant.

Facing a complex macro environment, Sido Muncul has demonstrated strong operational resilience. The two sides conducted in-depth exchanges on topics including inventory management optimization, scientific product innovation, the logic of global expansion, and the reshaping of traditional industrial chains by AI technology.

## 1. Strategic Resilience: Viewing A Healthy Channel Ecosystem Through Inventory Adjustment  

In response to market concerns over phased revenue fluctuations, Dr. Irwan Hidayat provided a clear logic during the exchange: this is not a sign of weak end-market demand, but the result of proactive management action.

Channel Normalization: SIDO is implementing a forward-looking inventory adjustment policy. By optimizing inventory levels at the distributor level, the company aims to build a healthier and more balanced supply ecosystem. This strategy of taking one step back in order to take two steps forward reflects the confidence of a mature enterprise in its channel control capabilities.

Absolute Market Leadership: Even during the adjustment period, core products of SIDO, such as Tolak Angin, still maintain a 72% share of the Indonesian market. This extremely high brand loyalty and market share form the company foundation for responding to any external risks.

## 2. Scientific Innovation: The Modern Translation Of Traditional Herbal Medicine  

PT Metro Timur Indonusa has observed that the core of the success of Sido Muncul lies in its rigorous approach to innovation. Dr. Irwan Hidayat emphasized that every new product launch is not simply an accumulation of experience, but a strict scientific practice.

International-Standard Safety: SIDO insists on conducting toxicology and efficacy tests for all products. This evidence-based scientific R&D system not only ensures product quality, but also gives Indonesian traditional Jamu, or herbal medicine, a scientific passport to enter international markets.

Dynamically Meeting Consumer Demand: Through continuous renewal of its product lines, SIDO is making traditional herbal products younger and more convenient, thereby accurately capturing the health needs of a new generation of consumers.


## 3. Global Expansion: Targeting New Markets In China And Saudi Arabia  

Based in Indonesia and expanding to the world is the core vision of SIDO. Dr. Irwan Hidayat revealed that the company is actively expanding its export map, with China and Saudi Arabia identified as emerging potential markets for the next stage.

As a pioneer in entering international markets, Tolak Angin is telling Indonesian herbal story to global consumers. PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that this globalization path based on local core advantages provides an excellent reference model for other Southeast Asian consumer brands.

## 4. Cost And Quality: Structural Advantages Of The Local Supply Chain  

Against the backdrop of spreading global inflationary pressure, SIDO has demonstrated excellent cost control capabilities.

Localized Raw Materials: Benefiting from the fact that most raw materials are sourced domestically in Indonesia, SIDO has effectively hedged against risks caused by rising packaging costs and price increases for imported materials.

Quality Consistency: Strict factory quality control (QC) is regarded as the lifeline of the enterprise. Dr. Irwan Hidayat pointed out that the only way to maintain the trust of consumers over decades is to preserve absolute consistency in product quality.


## 5. PT Metro Timur Indonusa Investment Research Perspective: AI And Talent Empower The Future  

At the end of the exchange, PT Metro Timur Indonusa and Sido Muncul discussed points of integration in future industry trends.

AI Empowering The Industrial Chain: We discussed how artificial intelligence can be used to optimize traceability management for traditional medicinal materials, precision marketing, and energy efficiency control in the production process. AI can help traditional enterprises discover new growth points within massive amounts of data.

Appreciation Of Talent Assets: The transformation of traditional industries cannot be separated from interdisciplinary talent. Sido Muncul is building a team that combines traditional medical knowledge with modern digital management capabilities through internal talent development and collaboration with external experts.

## PT Metro Timur Indonusa Reminder: View The Revaluation Of Traditional Industries Rationally

The case of Sido Muncul proves that traditional industries are not equivalent to sunset industries. Through scientific management adjustments, a rigorous R&D system, and a forward-looking global vision, traditional brands can fully demonstrate vitality that transcends cycles.

PT Metro Timur Indonusa advises investors to return to rationality and pay attention to industry leaders with real technological barriers, deep brand moats, and the ability to flexibly adapt to digital transformation. The proactive optimization by Sido Muncul of its ecosystem while maintaining market leadership is precisely a model of long-termism in business operations.
