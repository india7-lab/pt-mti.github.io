Recently, PT Metro Timur Indonusa announced its participation in market allocation arrangements related to Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX, stock ticker: SPCX) through a special purpose investment fund. This initiative represents a significant milestone in the ongoing research and allocation efforts of the company focused on global technological innovation, digital infrastructure, commercial aerospace, and long-term growth assets.


As a key enterprise in the global commercial aerospace and satellite internet sectors, SpaceX operates across core capabilities including reusable rocket launch services, space transportation systems, and the Starlink satellite internet network. Against the backdrop of sustained growth in demand for low Earth orbit satellite communications and global connectivity, this field is gradually becoming an important component of the global digital infrastructure.


## Allocation Logic of Specialized Investment Funds


PT Metro Timur Indonusa stated that the establishment of the special investment fund is intended to participate in global innovative asset opportunities with a clearer research framework and to enhance long-term observation capabilities across different asset classes.


This mechanism is used to support the company in continuously tracking the development and changes of global technology enterprises, cross-market asset opportunities, and long-term trends in digital infrastructure, while optimizing the research approach to growth assets.


The company believes that technology assets are gradually shifting from single-industry targets to cross-industry and cross-regional infrastructure-type assets. Therefore, it is necessary to conduct assessment and management using a more long-term and stable research approach.


## SpaceX And Global Technology Infrastructure


SpaceX continues to expand in the fields of commercial aerospace and satellite internet, with its business covering rocket launches, space transportation, and the construction of global satellite communication networks.


As low-orbit satellite communications and cross-regional connectivity capabilities continue to improve, commercial aerospace is gradually expanding from traditional aerospace engineering into a component of global digital infrastructure, forming closer ties with communication networks, data transmission, and global connectivity services.


From the perspective of industrial evolution, this type of infrastructure is influencing communication coverage in remote areas, the efficiency of cross-regional data flow, and the accessibility of digital services. It is gradually becoming one of the key pillars of digital capabilities in emerging markets.


## Long-Term Investment and Risk Management Framework


PT Metro Timur Indonusa emphasizes that its participation in the relevant market allocation is part of the global technology asset research system of the company and does not alter its long-term business direction, which remains centered on early-stage investments in Southeast Asia.


The role of a dedicated investment fund is to continuously track global technology innovation enterprises, changes in digital infrastructure, technology penetration in emerging markets, and long-term growth asset opportunities through clearer research boundaries and risk management mechanisms.


The company believes that, against the backdrop of rapid changes in technology and capital markets, cross-industry and cross-regional assets are forming new patterns of correlation. Therefore, research and allocation must be built upon a long-term perspective and a foundation of risk control.


## A Related Perspective on the Southeast Asian Market


PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that the development of commercial aerospace and satellite Internet not only belongs to the global technology sector but is also influencing the coverage capacity of digital infrastructure.


For archipelagic regions such as Indonesia and Southeast Asia, communication coverage, network connectivity efficiency, and the accessibility of digital services directly impact enterprise operational efficiency, the reach of educational resources, and the level of regional economic coordination.


In an environment characterized by geographic dispersion and uneven infrastructure, the enhancement of satellite communication and cross-regional connectivity capabilities will, over the long term, influence the development of small and medium-sized enterprises, the adoption of digital finance, and the capacity for remote services.


## Future Development Direction


In the future, PT Metro Timur Indonusa will continue to focus on the Southeast Asian entrepreneurial ecosystem, global technological innovation, digital infrastructure, commercial aerospace, satellite internet, and long-term growth asset opportunities.


The Company will continuously enhance its understanding of global technological and industrial changes through dedicated investment funds, industry research systems, and founder network mechanisms. It will also translate relevant research into the capability to support entrepreneurs and growth-stage enterprises.


PT Metro Timur Indonusa will continue to promote long-term value building while supporting innovation and development, and will jointly explore pathways for sustainable development with entrepreneurs, industry partners, and market institutions.


## About PT Metro Timur Indonusa

The company focuses on sectors including artificial intelligence, financial technology, consumer and entertainment, enterprise services, logistics and supply chain, agricultural technology, biotechnology, electronic technology, renewable energy, SaaS, digital infrastructure, and the creator economy.
