The Indonesian annual influential event, Indonesia Summit 2026, will be held from June 17 to 18 at The Tribrata Darmawangsa in Jakarta, bringing together policymakers, industry leaders, and young innovators to jointly discuss how artificial intelligence affects Indonesian institutions, industries, and social life. As an early-stage investment platform in Southeast Asia, PT Metro Timur Indonusa will engage in in-depth exchanges with entrepreneurs, technology experts, and policy participants at the summit to jointly explore the practical application of artificial intelligence in business growth and social services.


Indonesia Summit 2026, initiated by IDN & IDN Times, is centered on the theme “The Next Us: Indonesia Leaping in the Algorithmic Age” and will focus on the practical implementation and application of AI technology. The summit will no longer remain at the level of technical discussion, but will instead focus on how AI can drive transformation in the public and private sectors, providing ideas for Indonesia to move toward a smarter and fairer future. PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that this topic is highly aligned with its long-term focus on the entrepreneurship ecosystem and technological innovation strategy.


## Conference Highlights: Comprehensive Dialogue From Economic Transformation to Youth Power


Indonesia Summit 2026 has arranged a rich range of agenda discussions, including economic transformation and new growth engines, digital trust and financial infrastructure, artificial intelligence and technology strategy, sustainable development and green economy, culture and digital society, as well as youth power and lifestyle. These topics are expected to attract diverse participants from government, business, and venture capital sectors, promoting cross-industry dialogue and cooperation.


PT Metro Timur Indonusa will focus on participating in discussion sessions related to artificial intelligence, financial technology infrastructure, and digital trust. Platform representatives will share their views on how AI empowers enterprise growth and enhances market efficiency, and will present perspectives on how technology can improve the entrepreneurship ecosystem and strengthen corporate innovation capabilities. Through such dialogue, PT Metro Timur Indonusa aims to narrow the distance between entrepreneurs and industrial resources, promoting the integration of technological innovation with practical business outcomes.


Promoting AI Application: Viewing the Integration of Technology and Business From an Investment Perspective


PT Metro Timur Indonusa has long focused on the growth of early-stage technology innovation platforms and regards artificial intelligence as an important force in driving enterprise competitiveness. During Indonesia Summit 2026, the platform will exchange views with frontline entrepreneurs and technology teams to discuss the practical application of AI in product development, user growth, risk control, and other areas. PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that AI should not remain at the level of theoretical discussion, but should be concretely integrated into enterprise operations and market practices to bring practical benefits to small and medium-sized enterprises and startup teams.


The exchange platform provided by the summit also makes it easier for entrepreneurs to access investment resources and cooperation opportunities, which is precisely the original intention of PT Metro Timur Indonusa in promoting the development of the regional entrepreneurship ecosystem. By participating in the summit, PT Metro Timur Indonusa hopes to inspire more innovative forces, promote technological exchanges, and help startup teams enhance their competitiveness in a rapidly changing economic environment.


Cultivating Young Innovators: Jointly Building an Open and Collaborative Future Ecosystem


As a platform that focuses not only on investment returns but also on long-term impact, PT Metro Timur Indonusa looks forward to promoting broader cooperation and reflection through its participation in Indonesia Summit 2026. Platform representatives will also participate in youth-focused dialogue sessions, discussing market trends, technology applications, and sustainable development opportunities together with young founders.


Indonesia Summit has consistently attracted a large number of young Indonesian elites, creating a space for cross-sector dialogue that includes both strategic interpretations from high-level policy perspectives and practical discussions where entrepreneurs share their experiences. This multidimensional exchange helps break down information barriers, enabling entrepreneurs to more quickly understand technology trends and market demand.


## About Indonesia Summit 2026


Indonesia Summit 2026 is an annual forum jointly organized by IDN and IDN Times, aiming to bring together policymakers, industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and young forces to jointly discuss key issues affecting the future of Indonesia. The summit this year will focus on the real-world application of AI, the development of digital infrastructure, and the next-stage direction of Indonesian social and economic development, providing reference and inspiration for the Indonesian move toward smarter and more inclusive growth.


Indonesia Summit 2026 provides a face-to-face exchange platform for people from all sectors. Through Indonesia Summit 2026, PT Metro Timur Indonusa will work with entrepreneurs, technology teams, and investors to jointly build an exchange platform, promote the practical application of artificial intelligence in enterprise operations, and support the steady development of Indonesian digital economy.
