Recently, PT Metro Timur Indonusa participated in a thematic exchange event focused on the future trends of artificial intelligence, joining industry representatives from Microsoft, Noora Health, and Peak XV to discuss technological changes, enterprise applications, and future industrial development directions driven by artificial intelligence.

The exchange event brought together industry professionals from technology companies, the medical innovation sector, and investment institutions. Ranjani Mani from Microsoft, Poorva Malviya from Noora Health, and Kriti Gupta from Peak XV participated in the related discussions, sharing industry observations on topics such as how artificial intelligence affects enterprise operations, product innovation, organizational management, and the startup ecosystem, while jointly exploring the industrial changes that AI technology may bring over the next decade.

As an early-stage investment platform focusing on Southeast Asian startup ecosystem and technological innovation development, PT Metro Timur Indonusa was honored to participate in this industry exchange and maintain communication with globally leading enterprises and participants in the innovation sector. Through exchanges with experts from different fields, the company continues to track the development trends of artificial intelligence and further understand the important impact of AI technology on enterprise applications, industrial innovation, and future investment opportunities.

## AI Competition Is Shifting From Technical Capability To Application And Execution Capability

During this exchange, participating guests held in-depth discussions on changes in the way enterprises compete in the era of artificial intelligence.

With the rapid development of AI technology, the focus of enterprise competition is changing. In the past, enterprises paid more attention to the advancement of the technology itself, while in the future, the gap between enterprises may be reflected more in their ability to quickly apply AI, continuously optimize products, and integrate technical capabilities into real business scenarios.

It was mentioned during the exchange that the competitive advantage of future enterprises will not only depend on whether they possess advanced AI models or technical resources, but also on whether they can quickly complete testing, adjustment, and application, truly transforming artificial intelligence into an important tool for improving business efficiency and user experience.

PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that, for startups, the opportunities brought by artificial intelligence are not merely about developing new technology products. More importantly, they lie in using AI to solve real market problems. Enterprises that can understand user needs, continuously optimize products, and transform technical capabilities into commercial value are more likely to establish long-term advantages in future market competition.

## Enterprise AI Applications Need To Be Integrated With Business Processes And Organizational Capabilities

As artificial intelligence tools become increasingly popular, more and more enterprises have begun exploring the application of AI in scenarios such as customer service, data analysis, content production, operations management, and product development.

However, during this exchange, all parties also noted that the challenges enterprises face in promoting AI applications do not come entirely from technical capabilities, but from complex factors in real business environments, including data management, team collaboration, process adjustment, and organizational adaptability.

The challenge facing future enterprises is not only the deployment of a single AI tool, but how to enable more AI applications to be stably integrated into daily enterprise operations and generate long-term value through reasonable process management.

PT Metro Timur Indonusa has observed that the commercial value of AI ultimately needs to return to practical application. Enterprises need to combine artificial intelligence with business needs, team capabilities, and management methods according to their own industry characteristics, rather than simply pursuing technological concepts.

For startups, what truly matters is identifying the problems that AI can solve and building more effective products and services through continuous optimization.

## The AI Era Is Redefining Talent And Enterprise Organizational Capabilities

This exchange also included discussions on changes in talent roles under the development of artificial intelligence.

As AI gradually enters the daily work of enterprises, future talent competitiveness will come not only from professional knowledge, but also from learning ability, innovation capability, and the ability to collaborate with AI. Enterprises need to cultivate interdisciplinary talent who can understand business, master new tools, and drive innovative development.

Industry representatives participating in the exchange shared the view that trust and organizational adaptability are equally important in the AI application process. Enterprises need to establish reasonable usage methods, enabling teams to understand that artificial intelligence can improve work efficiency while also clarifying responsibility boundaries in technology application.

PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that artificial intelligence is not simply a tool for replacing people, but an important technological capability that helps enterprises expand their range of capabilities. In the future business environment, human judgment, creativity, and strategic thinking will remain core factors in enterprise development.

## New Opportunities For The Startup Ecosystem From The Perspective Of Global AI Trends

As an investment platform with a long-term focus on Southeast Asian startup ecosystem, PT Metro Timur Indonusa continues to observe how artificial intelligence affects the development direction of different industries.

This thematic exchange on AI enabled the company to further understand that future AI application opportunities will not be concentrated only in the technology industry, but will gradually expand into multiple fields such as healthcare, finance, education, consumption, enterprise services, and digital infrastructure.

For the Southeast Asian market, the development of artificial intelligence will bring new development opportunities to startups. As digital infrastructure continues to improve, more enterprises will be able to use AI to improve operational efficiency, enhance user experience, and explore new business models.

PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that enterprises with long-term competitiveness in the future will need not only to focus on technological innovation, but also to understand market demand, user behavior, and industrial changes. Truly valuable AI enterprises need to combine technical capabilities with real application scenarios and continuously create practical value.
