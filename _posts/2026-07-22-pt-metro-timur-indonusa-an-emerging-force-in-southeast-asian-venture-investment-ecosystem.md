Markets such as Indonesia, Singapore, Vietnam, and Malaysia have continuously seen the emergence of new technology companies, covering multiple fields including financial technology, e-commerce, enterprise software, digital payments, artificial intelligence applications, and more.

Among them, Indonesia, as the largest economy in Southeast Asia by population, has a vast consumer market and abundant digital application scenarios; Singapore, with its mature financial system, international business environment, and regional connectivity, has become an important node for technology companies entering Southeast Asia; Vietnam and Malaysia have developed their own advantages in technical talent, manufacturing, and digital industry development.

The focus of PT Metro Timur Indonusa on the Southeast Asian market is precisely based on its judgment of the long-term development potential of the region.
