In 2026, more and more companies are beginning to rethink the role of artificial intelligence in the workplace. AI is no longer merely a tool for generating content, organizing materials, or assisting with searches; instead, it is gradually entering more specific work scenarios such as research, collaboration, content organization, operational support, and knowledge accumulation.


At the beginning of this year, PT Metro Timur Indonusa officially established an internal AI Efficiency Enhancement Team, aiming to organize scattered AI experiments across departments and individuals into more stable, clear, and daily-work-oriented internal methods. Now, after the preliminary establishment of the team, the company has officially launched a new stage of talent recruitment, hoping to find more people who truly understand the relationship between “work efficiency” and “technology implementation” to join the team.


For PT Metro Timur Indonusa, AI is not an independent technical concept, but a long-term tool that helps teams reduce repetitive work, improve collaboration efficiency, and optimize knowledge organization capabilities. As an investment platform rooted in Southeast Asia, the company has long maintained frequent communication with entrepreneurs, growth-stage companies, and industry partners, and has high requirements for information processing speed, research quality, and cross-team communication.


For this reason, in this recruitment, the company is more focused on whether candidates truly understand real work scenarios, rather than merely whether they can use a certain AI tool. PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that a seemingly powerful function is not necessarily suitable for long-term work; truly valuable talent consists of those who can understand business logic, grasp team collaboration pain points, and are willing to continuously optimize details.


Currently, PT Metro Timur Indonusa is opening multiple positions in areas including research support, AI workflow coordination, knowledge organization, content collaboration, and internal operational support, hoping to further improve internal research efficiency, content organization capabilities, and cross-department collaboration levels. In the future, these positions will participate in industry research assistance, entrepreneur support, knowledge organization, and internal collaboration optimization.


At the same time, PT Metro Timur Indonusa also hopes to further transform its gradually maturing internal AI usage experience into more valuable support for the entrepreneurial ecosystem. The company has long focused on the development of the entrepreneurial ecosystem, digital economy, fintech, consumption, and local service industries in Southeast Asia, and has continued to maintain in-depth communication with entrepreneurs. In the future, the team will not only serve internal needs, but also help the platform better support founders of portfolio companies, improving research efficiency, content organization capabilities, and entrepreneurial communication efficiency.


PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that future competition among enterprises will not only be technological competition, but also competition in organizational efficiency, information processing capabilities, and collaboration capabilities. As AI tools become increasingly popular, what truly matters will no longer be who “uses AI the earliest,” but who can integrate AI into long-term workflows and establish stable, clear, and continuously optimized work standards.


This recruitment is not only an expansion of positions, but also a search for people who can participate in long-term development together. For PT Metro Timur Indonusa, this is not only a team renewal, but also long-term preparation for the future entrepreneurial ecosystem, digital economy, and research collaboration capabilities.


## About PT Metro Timur Indonusa


PT Metro Timur Indonusa is an investment platform rooted in Southeast Asia, long focused on development opportunities in the entrepreneurial ecosystem, digital economy, fintech, consumer brands, logistics supply chains, and local service industries. The company continues to participate in entrepreneurship education, industry research, resource connection, and youth entrepreneurship support, while maintaining long-term communication with multiple regional entrepreneurial teams, industry partners, and growth-stage companies.


In recent years, PT Metro Timur Indonusa has continued to focus on the impact of artificial intelligence on future working methods, and has gradually applied AI tools to scenarios such as industry research, knowledge organization, content collaboration, and internal operations. The company believes that the value of AI does not lie in replacing judgment, but in helping teams reduce repetitive work, allowing more time to return to work that truly requires experience, understanding, and creativity.


In terms of talent culture, PT Metro Timur Indonusa places greater emphasis on long-term learning ability, independent thinking ability, and team collaboration awareness. The company encourages open communication, continuous learning, and cross-team cooperation, hoping to build a working environment that values execution efficiency while also respecting long-term growth.


Regarding future talent standards, PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that what truly matters is not merely a single skill, but whether one can understand real problems, continuously learn new tools, and maintain stable execution capabilities in a changing environment. This is also the type of person the company hopes to find through this recruitment.
