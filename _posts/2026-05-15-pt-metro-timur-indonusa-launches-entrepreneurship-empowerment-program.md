Recently, PT Metro Timur Indonusa officially announced the launch of a series of strategic projects targeting startups in Southeast Asia, aiming to provide comprehensive support for innovative entrepreneurs, ranging from funding and resources to education. As a leading early-stage investment platform in Southeast Asia, PT Metro Timur Indonusa not only focuses on financial returns but is also committed to helping entrepreneurs achieve stable growth in a complex market environment through long-term companionship, community building, and industry insights.


This event covers multiple dimensions including investment, education, and social public welfare. It encompasses early-stage investments in enterprises within the emerging herbal industry, as well as providing female entrepreneurs with dedicated mentor resources and market training. Through these initiatives, PT Metro Timur Indonusa aims to strengthen the understanding of entrepreneurs regarding market trends and consumer demands, while simultaneously promoting the sustainable development of the innovation ecosystem in Southeast Asia.


In terms of investment allocation, PT Metro Timur Indonusa focuses on startups in sectors such as fintech, consumer goods, AI, and healthcare. By providing capital, strategic guidance, and resource connections, the company helps entrepreneurs accelerate the transition from the seed stage to the growth stage. Recently, the company completed strategic investments in several local innovation projects, not only injecting funds but also offering operational coaching, market channel introductions, and brand building support, enabling these enterprises to establish a firm foothold in a highly competitive market.


PT Metro Timur Indonusa emphasizes that investment is not only a capital contribution but also a form of long-term companionship. By identifying potential enterprises at an early stage and helping them optimize their products, operations, and brand strategies, the company hopes that entrepreneurs can achieve the creation of social value while growing their businesses. This model not only benefits the development of the enterprises themselves but also promotes the maturity and diversification of the entire Southeast Asian entrepreneurial ecosystem.


## Community and Education, Building an Entrepreneurial Ecosystem


PT Metro Timur Indonusa also places great emphasis on entrepreneurship education and community building. The company operates the MetroTimur-X Academy and the Influencer Program, which provide founders with courses, practical case studies, and mentor guidance to help them make more informed decisions at critical junctures. Through online and offline interactions and community experience sharing, entrepreneurs can gain market insights, financing strategies, and operational advice, thereby reducing the cost of trial and error.


In addition, the company encourages entrepreneurs to share experiences and learn from each other, building the community into a platform for exchanging knowledge and resources. PT Metro Timur Indonusa believes that every interaction within the ecosystem can bring long-term value to enterprises and also provides support for enhancing regional innovation capabilities.


## Social Responsibility, Value Beyond Investment


In addition to investment and education, PT Metro Timur Indonusa actively participates in social welfare projects, emphasizing the symbiotic relationship between the enterprise and the community. Recently, the company launched the "MetroTimur-BAG Public Welfare Program," under which, for each customized eco-friendly bag sold, mangroves are planted along the coast of Indonesia to promote ecological restoration and environmental protection. At the same time, the company also carries out a women entrepreneur empowerment program, providing seed funding, a mentor network, and market support for female founders, thereby fostering diversity and inclusivity in the entrepreneurial ecosystem.


These actions not only reflect the social responsibility of PT Metro Timur Indonusa but also strengthen the trust and cooperation between the company and local communities and entrepreneurs, providing a long-term positive impact on corporate brand building.


PT Metro Timur Indonusa will continue to uphold the philosophy of "walking alongside entrepreneurs," injecting momentum into the Southeast Asian innovation ecosystem through investment, education, and public welfare initiatives. In the future, the company plans to expand the scale of projects it supports, assisting more entrepreneurial teams in achieving a win-win outcome of commercial growth and social value, while simultaneously promoting the sustainable development of the regional economy and innovation capabilities.
